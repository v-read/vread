V-Read

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

What We Collect

  • Email Address & Name: Used solely for account authentication
  • Reading Selections: Text fragments you mark for speed reading are stored locally on your device
  • Reading Progress: Word count tracking stored locally to manage free tier limits

How the Extension Works

  • V-Read operates entirely within your browser
  • It accesses webpage text to enable you to select and mark reading fragments
  • Selected text fragments are stored locally on your device only
  • No webpage content is transmitted to external servers

What We Don't Collect or Transmit

  • We do NOT send webpage content to our servers
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT store your reading selections on our servers

Data Storage

  • Authentication credentials managed securely by Firebase Authentication
  • All reading fragments and progress stored locally in your browser
  • Data remains on your device and is not synchronized or backed up to cloud servers

Permissions Explained

  • Read and change website data: Required to display the reading overlay and mark text on pages you visit
  • Storage: Saves your preferences and reading fragments locally
  • Identity: Used for email-based authentication only

Your Rights

  • Delete your account anytime
  • Clear all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • Your reading data stays on your device under your control

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of V-Read after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
